AALIA TECH

Aalia est un assistant vocal

Notre outil de traduction est un chatbot vocal qui interprète en temps réel, une conversation entre un professionnel de santé et son patient

Notre Solution

AALIA, facilite une traduction médicale

Les buts : Faciliter la communication entre un professionnel de sante et un patient qui ne parlent pas la meme langue. Réintégrer les patients dans le parcours de soin. Désengorger tous les services medicaux freinés par les problèmes de compréhension de langage.

Availability

The tool can be used every day, at any time of the day, both for your consultations and for emergencies.

Privé

No need for a third party,communication is only between the caregiver and the patient. Thus, medical confidentiality is well guarded.

Contextualized

More than a classic translation tool, Aalia is able to interpret the conversation taking into account the medical context.

Fiabilites

The history of the conversation is presented to the doctor in real time so that he can know exactly what was understood and interpreted by the Aalia assistant during the medical interview with his patient.

Qualités

Our translations are provided by healthcare professionals with a dual cultural background and knowledge of both the medical and cultural context of the patient.
